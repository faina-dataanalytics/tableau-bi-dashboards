# Product Analytics Dashboards (Tableau)

This repository contains two interactive Tableau dashboards focused on product and revenue analytics.

## Project Overview

The goal of this project was to design executive-level dashboards that support data-driven decision-making in digital product environments.

The repository includes:

Mobile Game Analytics Dashboard

Revenue & Business Analytics Dashboard

Both dashboards were built with a strong focus on KPI modeling, segmentation, and interactive business reporting.

## 1. Mobile Game Analytics
### Objective

Analyze user engagement and product feature usage in a mobile game.

### Key Metrics

Monthly Active Users (MAU)

Average Playtime per User (HH:MM)

Battle Pass Adoption Rate

Age Group Segmentation

Seasonal Activity Trends

### Core Insights

Active users increase throughout the year with mild seasonal fluctuations.

High average playtime indicates strong engagement.

Battle Pass is used by a smaller but stable segment.

### Business Impact

Enables product teams to monitor engagement independently.

Identifies high-performing user segments.

Supports monetization strategy decisions.

## 2. Revenue & Business Analytics
### Objective

Analyze revenue development and monetization performance across products and regions.

### Key Metrics

Total Revenue

Paid Users

ARPPU (Average Revenue per Paying User)

Regional Performance

Revenue Cohort Analysis (LTV perspective)

### Core Insights

Overall revenue shows steady growth.

The USA is the strongest revenue driver.

APAC region gains importance over time.

Paid users increase while ARPPU slightly declines.

Cohort analysis reveals predictable but stable revenue decay over time.

### Business Impact

Clear visibility of revenue drivers.

Comparability across regions and products.

Supports executive-level strategic decisions.
